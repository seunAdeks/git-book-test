## Taco Recipe

### Ingredients
For the meat:

1 lb ground beef (or turkey)
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon paprika
½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
Salt and pepper to taste
½ cup water
For assembly:

Taco shells or tortillas
Shredded lettuce
Diced tomatoes
Shredded cheese
Sour cream
Salsa

### Instructions
Prepare the meat: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened (about 3 minutes). Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.

Brown the meat: Add ground beef to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until browned (about 5-7 minutes). Drain excess fat if needed.

Add seasoning: Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the meat.

Simmer: Add water and simmer for 5 minutes until the mixture thickens slightly.

Warm shells: Heat taco shells according to package directions (typically in the oven at 350°F for 5 minutes).

Assemble tacos: Fill each shell with the seasoned meat and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.

**Serve**: Serve immediately while warm. Makes about 8-10 tacos.
